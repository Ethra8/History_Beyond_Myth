Coincidences & Curiosities

This site is dedicated to investigate true history facts, throughtly documented from unvaluable digitalized manuscripts from the National Library of the United Kingdom (British Library), and other valuable and reliable resources, such as Cambridge University research papers, or the Encyclopaedia Britannica, amongst others.

Its mission is to present the curious reader with coincidences found, after years of research, between true history facts, and a well-known myth, such as the Arthurian legends.

However, in any case does it seek to confirm these curious coincidences found as the ultimate truth behind the myth, although it is frequently stated by historians that there is often a part of hidden history behing every myth...
