About us

This site is dedicated to investigate and expose curious coincidences found, during years of investigation, between academically recognised historical facts, and myths such as the Arthurian legends.

All facts exposed are throughtly referenced, and documented from unvaluable digitalized manuscripts from the National Library of the United Kingdom (British Library), and other valuable and reliable resources, such as Cambridge University research papers, or the Encyclopaedia Britannica, amongst others.

However, in any case does it seek to confirm these curious coincidences found as the ultimate truth behind the myth, although it is frequently stated by historians that there is often a part of hidden history behing every myth...
